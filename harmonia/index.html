Cultural Insights

Mayan Art & Architecture

The Mayans are known for their magnificent stone structures, including the famous step pyramids and palaces, which continue to awe visitors today.

The Mayan Calendar

One of the most famous achievements of the Maya is their calendar system, which is one of the most accurate ancient timekeeping systems ever devised.

Mayan Language & Writing

The Maya developed a sophisticated writing system known as hieroglyphs, which was used to record historical events, rituals, and important information.

Cuisine and traditions

The Mayan diet was rich in corn, beans, and squash. Their culinary traditions continue to influence modern cuisine in the region.

Art & Craftsmanship

The Mayans were expert artisans, creating intricate pottery, jewelry, and murals that tell stories of their daily life, gods, and rulers.
